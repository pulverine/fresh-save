"Save Money and Reduce Food Waste"

Fresh Save is a system designed to help you save money and reduce food waste with products that keep food fresh, a recipe generator, a free food marketplace, and a barcode scanning feature that provides information about compatibility and proper storage, all promoting a sustainable and equitable food system.

    • Features

    • SCAN FOOD ITEMS

      • Users can scan the barcode on food items and find out important information about it such as the brand name, expiry date, the date of manufacture, and others.

    • FOOD ITEMS INVENTORY

      • Users can view what food products they have stored or scanned and their use by dates. Fresh save helps users keep track of the expiry dates of food items to prevent wastage.

    • FREE FOOD COMMUNITIES

      • Users can give out or receive food items to free food communities. Users also have the option to sell off bulk food items close to their expiry dates at a bargain price to reduce food wastage.
